Hi, I'm Stefan Lembo-Stolba.

I study data journalism at Columbia University.

Before grad-school I studied journalism and politics in Albany, NY, and worked at several media outlets in the New York capitol. My work has appeared in the Albany Times Union, the New York Daily News and on NPR's website. I now focus on using data to report investigative stories; and am learning to use Python and other programming tools to creatively report on cool projects.
